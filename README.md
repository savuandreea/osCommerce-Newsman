# Newsman plugin for osCommerce & Remarketing

Newsman plugin for osCommerce connects your store with Newsman for customer sync, newsletter subscribers, remarketing, ecommerce tracking, and product feed export.

Use it to sync customers and subscribers to lists or segments, send ecommerce events from the storefront, and keep product data available for marketing campaigns.

## Features

- Customer and subscriber sync
- Product feed export
- Remarketing and ecommerce tracking
- Frontend ecommerce events:
  - product view
  - cart view
  - add to cart
  - remove from cart
  - purchase

## Installation

Copy the `NewsMAN` directory into:

```text
lib/common/extensions/NewsMAN/
```

Then open the module in the osCommerce admin area, click `Edit`, and enable the needed options such as remarketing, ecommerce events, customer sync, or product feed.
